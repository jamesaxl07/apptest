davao ang pogi ng amerikano

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 Cup cornstarch
  • 3/4 Cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 Cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 Cup sesame seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl: flour, cornstarch, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Mix well.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together milk, melted butter, and egg. Stir until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Mix well to form a dough.
  4. Scoop a tablespoon of dough, form it into a ball, and roll it in sesame seeds.
  5. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Ensure the oil is hot before adding thes dough.
  6. Deep fry the balls until golden brown. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
